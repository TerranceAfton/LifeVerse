# LifeVerse Development Log #001

**Date:** July 6, 2026

**Milestone:** Character Foundation Complete

---

## Summary

Today's development session marked the completion of the Character Foundation milestone for LifeVerse. The focus was on finalizing the third-person player experience and preparing the project for interactive gameplay.

The player can now move freely using a camera-relative movement system while a fully functional third-person camera follows the character. The character hierarchy was reorganized for better scalability, and the project was migrated completely to Unity's New Input System.

This milestone establishes the gameplay foundation that future systems such as furniture interactions, object interactions, and NPC gameplay will build upon.

## Challenges

The biggest challenge was correctly synchronizing camera movement with player movement. Several iterations were required before camera-relative controls behaved consistently regardless of camera rotation.

Additional time was spent improving the project's architecture to ensure future gameplay systems could be integrated cleanly.
